Hello,

I am a postdoctoral research fellow seeking a position in mathematical biology. I obtained a PhD in applied mathematics from Comenius University in Bratislava, Slovakia under the supervision of Pavol Bokes. During my doctoral studies, I specifically focused on stochastic models of gene expression. My thesis is entitled “Multivariate Markovian models of biological processes”.

My research interests lie in the intersection of differential equations, stochastic processes and biochemical systems.
